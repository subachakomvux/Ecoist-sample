What is Ecoist ?

Ecoist is a new solution and product that is closing the gap between car and motorbike. It’s an electric three-wheeler for two people, getting you 100km and with the maximum speed of 100km/h so you can drive in traffic on country roads.

Thomas Koch, is the founder of Ecoist. An interview with him which will explain the vision of the company

What is Ecoist?

Ecoist started when I was living in Gothenburg commuting in a heavy 5-seated car all by myself, like everybody else did and still do. It’s such a waste of natural resources and energy. I felt a strong urge to do something about it.

What is Lean Clean Green for you ?

For me it’s important to take care of this planet and to use the available resources with a great responsibility. A product should be as compact as possible as long as it can solve the customers problem and keep the user happy – the product should be used a lot, and for as long as possible before being recycled.

What are your thoughts about the future industry for electric vehicles?

My thoughts about the EV industry is divided. There are a lot of advantages to not release exhausts in cities and where people are living. The driving experience is great! Instant torque makes electric vehicles fun to drive and traveling quieter helps towns and drivers to get a calmer environment.
